# 🚀 ApexEdge – Future Skills Academy

ApexEdge is a modern educational platform designed to prepare students for the careers of tomorrow. The website showcases an institution that goes beyond traditional education by focusing on practical, industry-relevant skills such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Programming, Entrepreneurship, Finance, Communication Skills, and Career Development.

## 🌐 Live Demo

**Website:** https://6a4580f19f1e5123d39faa2a--merry-kheer-2e74e3.netlify.app/

---

## 📖 About the Project

Traditional education often emphasizes theory, while today's industries demand practical skills and continuous learning. ApexEdge bridges this gap by presenting a platform where students can learn the technologies and life skills needed to succeed in the modern world.

The website is designed with a clean, responsive, and engaging interface to provide visitors with a professional experience across all devices.
